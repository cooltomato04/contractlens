## Auto-renewal traps
Contracts that renew automatically with less than 30 days 
notice period are considered predatory. Standard is 60–90 days.
Example language to flag: "shall automatically renew unless 
terminated with 7 days written notice"

## Unilateral amendment
Any clause allowing one party to change terms without consent 
is high risk.
Flag language: "reserves the right to amend these terms at any time"

## Overbroad non-compete
Non-compete exceeding 1 year duration or national scope is 
unusual for standard employment in Malaysia.
