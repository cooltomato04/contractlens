## Indemnification
What it means: You agree to pay the other party's legal costs 
and damages if something goes wrong.
What to check: Is it mutual (both sides) or one-sided?
Red flag: "Employee shall indemnify Company against all claims 
arising from..."

## Governing Law
What it means: Which country's courts handle disputes.
What to check: Should be Malaysia if you're a Malaysian entity.
Red flag: Governing law set to a foreign jurisdiction with 
no Malaysian operations.
